Week 12: 11.12

Revise your proposals

  • Extend your proposals to 750 words by adding new and refined ideas from the workshop session
  • Expand on your improved ideas of message, content, and aesthetic.
  • Add demarcated sections for:
    • Audience consideration: How will you select/create content to communite to your specific audience. How will you design for them?
    • Assets: What images, text, content do you already have and what do you still need?
    • Criteria for success: What guidlines can the final product be evaluated against to determine if you accomplished what was proposed?

Create your content

  • Start writing your message and gathering your content. You may want to take photographs, scan things, find media or information online, etc. This should be an active process of authorship and research.
