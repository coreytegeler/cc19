Week 6: 10.01

Assignment: Collaging on the Web

Scrapbooking: Collect images and quotes over the week and lay them out as a scrapbook collage.
-or-
Worldbuilding: Use images and HTML elements to construct your own virtual place.
-or-
Frankenstein’s Monster: Stitch together images and pieces of HTML to bring a new creature to life.
  • Use images, text, and other HTML elements to create a collage style webpage.
  • You can design a sketch on paper, Illustrator, or Photoshop and try to “translate” it to the web.
  • Use different HTML tags with shared classes and unique IDs.
  • Use the CSS size and positioning skills we learned to create your layout.
    • width, height, position (top, bottom, left, right), z-index, display, float, transform, etc.
