Week 9: 10.22

Assignment: Website as an ecosystem

Create a website full of living things. These things are born, they die, they reproduce, they move, they evolve. The user plays god.
  • Begin with a page full of shapes, images, text, etc.
  • Use event listeners (click, mouseover, mouseleave, resize, scroll) to add new elements, duplicate ones that already exist, and change the class or ID of elements to affect their styles.
  • Create at least 5 different types of interactions/effects.
  • The site doesn’t need to serve any purpose other than being fun to use.
